Who are you?  
You masquerader.  
A leaf formation? A plant? A flower?  
Tell me and tell me now, oh deceiver.  

I am me  
Whatever I want to be.  
Your names are limited  
by what you want to see.  
The deception, one and only,  
Is your perception.
